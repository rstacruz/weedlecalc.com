Pokemon Go power-leveling

The most efficient way to gain experience points in Pokemon Go is to save all your evolutions and do them while you use a Lucky Egg. Unfortunately, a Lucky Egg only lasts for 30 minutes. Use this calculator to make the most of your time!

  1. Don't evolve any Pokemon. Save up all your evolvable Pokemons until you have a Lucky Egg!

  2. Acquire a lucky egg. You will get one when you reach level 8, 9, 10, 15. You will get two on level 20. (reference)

  3. Once you have enough evolvables (around 150 Pidgey candies), use this calculator to get step-by-step instructions on how to make the most of your Lucky Egg.
